Welcome to my music page! Here I'll link songs and projects that I've worked on sorted by style. None of it is on Spotify or anything, this is just for fun.

SiIvaGunner Style Rips

This are remixes and mashups of (mostly) video game and anime music. Most of them are categorically bad, but I think they're amusing. They are largely (entirely) inspired by channels like SiIvaGunner and Chongo. If you like these, I highly recommend checking them out!
